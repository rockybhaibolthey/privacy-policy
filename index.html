Privacy Policy for Cyclopedia Scrap Pickup Service

Last Updated: January 28, 2025

Version 1.0

At Cyclopedia, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy and protecting the personal information you provide when using our scrap pickup services. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, share, and protect your data in compliance with applicable laws. By using our platform and services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Collection of Information

When you use our scrap pickup service, we collect various types of information to facilitate your request, improve our services, and ensure smooth communication between you and our team. Below is an overview of the information we collect and how it is used:

Personal Information

Mobile Number: Upon installing the app, you will be prompted to enter your mobile number for verification purposes. This helps us ensure the security of your account, stay connected with you for service-related updates, and assist you with any queries you may have.

Location Information

Address: You are required to provide a delivery or pickup address, which can be manually entered by you or automatically retrieved via GPS if you choose to enable location services. We collect your latitude and longitude coordinates along with the corresponding address to help our team provide accurate and timely pickup services. You have the option to enable or disable location access at any time through your device settings.

Pickup Details

Pickup Information: When scheduling a pickup, you may provide additional details such as:

  • Date and Time: To schedule the pickup at a convenient time for you.
  • Type of Recyclables: The category or type of scrap you wish to dispose of (e.g., plastic, paper, metal).
  • Weight of Scrap: The estimated or actual weight of the recyclables (in kilograms) to assist in calculating compensation.
  • Supermarket Pickup (Optional): If you request a pickup from a supermarket, you will need to provide the supermarket name and other relevant details necessary to arrange the pickup service.

Payment Information

Payment Methods: Payments can be made in cash at the time of pickup or via third-party digital payment methods such as UPI or mobile wallets.

Transaction Reference: If the payment is made via third-party payment methods, we store the transaction ID as a reference to track the payment for our records. However, we do not collect or store any personal payment details such as credit card numbers, bank account information, or digital wallet credentials. All transactions are completed at the time of pickup, either in cash or through these third-party digital payment methods.

Service History and Records

We may store pickup records for each transaction, including the details of the recyclable items collected, the date of service, and the amount of money exchanged. This helps us maintain a service history for future reference and improve the quality of our service.

2. How We Use the Information We Collect

We use the information we collect for several purposes, including:

  • To Provide Services: The data you provide is used to schedule and manage scrap pickups, verify your identity, calculate scrap weight and corresponding payment, and facilitate communication between you and our service team.
  • For Communication: Your mobile number will be used to send updates regarding your scheduled pickups, confirm pickup times, or notify you of any changes to the service.
  • To Improve Our Services: We use the data we collect to analyze trends, improve our offerings, and ensure that pickups are performed efficiently and in a timely manner.
  • For Legal and Compliance Purposes: We may retain records for future reference, such as resolving disputes, ensuring service accuracy, and complying with local regulations.

3. Sharing of Information

We share your information with our in-house team or pickup partners who assist us in performing the pickup services. This ensures that your service is carried out efficiently. The information shared may include:

  • Pickup details, including the address and type of recyclables.
  • Service history and records for processing pickups.

We do not share your personal information with other third parties, except where required by law or to facilitate the performance of the service as outlined in this policy.

4. Security of Your Personal Information

We are committed to protecting your personal information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, destruction, and alteration. We use reasonable security measures, including encryption, firewalls, and secure servers, to protect your data in compliance with applicable laws. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and while we make every effort to protect your data, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

When you access or change your account or personal information, we offer a secure connection using a secure server to ensure the safety of your data. You are also responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information and taking adequate precautions on your end to protect your account from unauthorized access.

5. Retention of Your Personal Information

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by applicable law. We may retain records for service history, dispute resolution, or compliance with legal obligations. Once the data is no longer required, we will remove or de-identify it to ensure it is no longer personally identifiable.

6. Disclosure of Your Personal Data

Business Transactions

If Cyclopedia is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your personal data may be transferred. We will provide notice before your personal data is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy.

Law Enforcement

Under certain circumstances, Cyclopedia may be required to disclose your personal data if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

7. Your Consent, Rights, and Changes to Privacy Notice

Your Consent

By using our platform and services, you consent to the collection, processing, and storage of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. You may withdraw your consent at any time by visiting the 'Settings' section of the app. Please note that withdrawal of consent may limit your ability to use some features of the service, such as scheduling a pickup.

Right to Erasure and Withdrawal of Consent

You have the right to request that we erase your personal information. If you wish to withdraw your consent or delete your account, please visit Home Page > Settings Icon > Profile in the mobile application. Requests will be processed within 24 hours. Once your account is deleted, all associated data will be removed from our system, and you will no longer be able to use our services.

Modification of Personal Information

You can update or correct your personal information through the mobile application at any time. Please note that your phone number cannot be modified once verified. If you need to change your phone number, please contact our support team for assistance.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us at legal@zeptonow.com. We will make reasonable efforts to respond to your inquiries in a timely manner. Please note that we can only respond to queries related to this Privacy Policy and privacy practices.

9. Data Storage and Third-Party Services

Your personal information is stored on secure servers located in India. We may use cloud services for storage and processing, including services provided by Amazon Web Services (AWS). These services have their own security and privacy measures in place. You can review their security measures and privacy policy at AWS Security and AWS Privacy Policy.

10. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant changes via email or through a prominent notice on our platform. Please review the Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

11. Legal Compliance

We comply with all applicable privacy laws in India, including the Personal Data Protection Bill (PDPB) and relevant regulations. Your rights and obligations under these laws are protected in line with our practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

12. Network Permissions and Data Usage

As part of our Cyclopedia Scrap Pickup Service, we require certain permissions to provide a seamless user experience:

  • Internet Access: Our app requires android.permission.INTERNET to access the internet for placing orders and retrieving updates securely.
  • Network State Access: We request android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE to check the availability of an internet connection on your device, ensuring that you are notified if a network connection is unavailable.
